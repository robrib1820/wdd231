Site Name

Energyn

Thinking about the name of my website, I want to demonstrate something that brings strength, energy, and a quality of life by doing workouts. So I thought of combining the words "energy" with "gym," and that's what we got. energyn.online

Site purpose

The website will provide instructions for gym beginners, including specific workouts for each body part and nutrition options for optimal performance and body development. There will be a form to register where people can receive specialized treatment with a nutritionist or even request a personal trainer.

Scenarios

How long should my workout be?

How many exercises should I do, number of sets, and how much rest time?

Can I do strength training and aerobic exercise together?

Should I eat before or after training?
